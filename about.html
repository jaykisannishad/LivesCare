About Us

At LivesCare, we believe that every life is valuable and deserves to be cared for. As an NGO, we are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of people in need. Our organization was founded on the principles of compassion, equality, and justice. We believe that by working together and supporting one another, we can create a better world for everyone. At LivesCare, we focus on providing aid to those who need it most. We offer a range of services, including food and clothes assistance, healthcare support, and education books and care. We are proud to work with individuals and communities across the globe to address issues of poverty, inequality, and injustice. Our team is made up of dedicated and passionate individuals who are committed to making a difference. We are grateful for the support of our donors and volunteers, who help us to continue our mission of caring for lives. We invite you to join us in our efforts to create a more compassionate and just world. Whether through donations, volunteering, or spreading the word, your support can make a significant impact. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of people in need.
