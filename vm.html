Our Vission and mission

Welcome to LivesCare, an NGO dedicated to improving the lives of people in need through the collection and distribution of donations. Our mission is to alleviate suffering and empower individuals and communities by providing them with the basic necessities of life, such as food, clothes and healthcare. At LivesCare, we believe that everyone deserves to live a life of dignity and that every little effort counts towards creating a better world. With the support of generous donors like you, we can make a significant impact in the lives of those who need it most. Our vision is a world where no one goes hungry or without education, and where everyone has access to quality healthcare. We envision a society that is compassionate, inclusive, and just, where everyone has the opportunity to reach their full potential. Join us in our mission to make the world a better place. Donate to LivesCare today and help us make a difference in the lives of people in need.
